About Me

Hi, my name is Kerrin and I am a junior developer currently based in Edinburgh, UK.

I have spent my career so far working as a live event producer in the music industry. Working with my team, I have delivered everything from small concerts to arena shows and large scale outdoor festivals.

I have always had an interest in technology, but it was only when I began to learn programming last year that I realised this is what I want to be doing professionally.

So far my focus has been web development using JavaScript and React, but I have recently been learning Python and have just began a course in PHP. I mainly learn through project based learning, Udemy courses and coding challenges like Leetcode and Codewars. I am currently available for work, and looking to join a company where I can grow as a developer and contribute my skills to the team.

Since the pandemic, you can now find me stepping away from music, volunteering at animal rescues and spending all my spare time coding. I hope you can get to know me a little through this portfolio. Please feel free to get in touch with me via email or the form below.
