About Me

Hi, my name is Kerrin and I am a self taught junior developer currently based in London, UK.

I have spent my career so far working as an events producer in the music industry. Working with my team, I have delivered everything from small concerts to arena shows and large scale outdoor festivals.

I have always had an interest in technology, but it was only when I began to learn programming last year that I realised this is what I want to be doing.

So far my focus has been web development, but I have recently discovered my love for Python, so I have been trying to improve my skills in this area. I mainly learn through online resources, Udemy courses and coding challenges like HackerRank and Codewars. I am currently available for work, and looking to join a company where I can grow as a developer and contribute my skills.

Since the pandemic, you can now find me covered in mud, caring for the animals at my local sanctuary and spending all my spare time coding. I hope you feel that you can get to know me through this portfolio.
